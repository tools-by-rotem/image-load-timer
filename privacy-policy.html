Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to the privacy policy for Image Load Timer. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and this page explains how we handle your information.

Data Collection

Image Load Timer does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The extension operates entirely locally on your browser, ensuring full privacy for all users.

Usage

The extension processes image load times directly on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties.
